C-130 and KC-135 Flight Coming Up

The Ormond Beach Composite Squadron will be flying on a C-130 and a KC-135 by the end of the year. They will be on two seperate dates. The C-130 flight will be out of Patrick AFB, and the KC-135 flight out of MacDill AFB.
